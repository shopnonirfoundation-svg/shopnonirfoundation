About Us

The SHOPNIR Foundation is dedicated to uplifting marginalized communities by providing access to quality education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihoods. Our mission is to foster empowerment and create lasting change through community-driven initiatives.

Founded in 2020, we have impacted thousands of lives through our programs in rural and urban areas, focusing on equity, inclusion, and sustainable development.
