About Us

On April 14, 2024 (1st Boishakh 1431, Bangali), a group of enthusiastic young people from Nidtolaj (Atani and Dhingpara) areas, with the cooperation of local residents, established an organization through a courtyard meeting. This platform aims to work selflessly to ensure the social security of all people in the Nidtolaj (Atani and Dhingpara) area.

Founded in 2020, we have impacted thousands of lives through our programs in rural and urban areas, focusing on equity, inclusion, and sustainable development.
